Tic Tac Toe

This fourth game was written in JavaScript. I took the information I learned from the past games and coded a tic tack toe game completely from scratch The objective of the game is just to win tic tac toe.








Use the SHOOT button to shoot a blast from your tank and use the UP and DOWN buttons to direct your shot. When your shot hits a red wall, both the shot and the wall will disapear. Don't let your tank hit a wall! To play again, press the RESET button, and then press START.
